Where do bananas come from?

Bananas originated in the Malay Archipelago in Southeast Asia. Today they are grown in tropical regions across the globe, from South and Central America to India, China and Africa

Why do monkeys like bananas ?

It's probably because bananas tend to grow in the hot, tropical areas where monkeys usually live. The bananas we eat are too sweet for monkeys and are only good as occasional treats.
